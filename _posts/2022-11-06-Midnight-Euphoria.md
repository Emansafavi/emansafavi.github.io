## Setup

Midnight Euphoria is a home-studio performance built around a VCV Rack patch, guitar, and live controller mapping.

<p align="center">
  <img src="{{ '/assets/images/midnight-euphoria-cover.jpg' | relative_url }}" alt="Midnight Euphoria Cover Art" style="width:60%; max-width:400px; border-radius:12px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:20px;">
</p>

The patch was designed to handle drums, arpeggios, and pads while I played over it live.

Main elements:

- A drum machine for pulse
- Arpeggiators and sequenced material
- Ambient pad layers
- Guitar and occasional synth on top
- MIDI control for switching and shaping the patch

Patch: [Midnight Euphoria on Patchstorage](https://patchstorage.com/midnight-ephoria/)
