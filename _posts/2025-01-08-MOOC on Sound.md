This post is a reference page for the *Experiment with Sounds* MOOC.

It is not meant as a full essay or course summary. I use it mainly as a compact public note listing some of the artists, researchers, and topics that came up during the course.

## Topics

- sound studies
- listening and silence
- improvisation
- field recording
- sound and architecture
- rhythm, timbre, and duration
- streaming and studio practice
