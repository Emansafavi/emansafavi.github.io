Violet Bubbles is a VCV Rack patch and one-take performance from December 2022.

## The Patch
I built this patch in **VCV Rack (free version)**. It is structured around:

- A quantized sequence running through two oscillators
- A simple drum machine with delayed reflections
- A live-played bass part on a MIDI controller
- Delayed and modulated layers added over time
- A form that begins and ends in noise

This recording was made quickly and left mostly as it was:
