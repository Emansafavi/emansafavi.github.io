In August 2025 I worked with dancer and performer Awa Jalali on *UN03*, presented at dbstudio in Berlin.

I produced the soundscape for the piece.

### Structure

The work combined two main elements:

- an opening sound collage built from found aircraft recordings
- a narrated voice track processed through stretching, layering, and timing shifts

The main challenge was keeping the voice present while letting it drift in and out of clarity.

The final soundscape ran for roughly 20 minutes.
