## Mythologizer

Mythologizer is a collaborative research project developed in 2024 by Lilli-Chiara Kurth, Célestin Meunier, Eman Safavi Bayat, and Pierre-Louis Suckrow.

The project looked at how myths change across time, context, and retelling. It combined workshops, interviews, a simple analogue game, and a digital simulation model.

## What the project included

- An analogue myth-making game
- Recorded interviews and workshop sessions
- A short film
- A website for tracking how stories changed
- An agent-based simulation for testing myth transmission over many iterations
