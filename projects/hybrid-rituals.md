# Hybrid Rituals

Hybrid Rituals was developed for *An Experimental Ecology* at UNI.T Theater, UdK Berlin, on February 8, 2025.

The piece combined motion tracking, projection, light, and sound in a live performance setting. Audience proximity and movement affected the behaviour of the system, so the stage shifted between choreographed action and responsive environment.

## My contribution

- Sound design
- Concept development
- Research
- Quick prototyping
- Performance

## Project frame

The work grew out of collaboration inside the InKüLe context and was shown as part of the UdK 50-year anniversary program. The project sat somewhere between performance, installation, and technical experiment.

What mattered most was building a setup that could stay readable in a live setting. The technology was there to support the atmosphere and the interaction, not to dominate it.
