## LOOPS Series

LOOPS is an event series produced within [NewPractice](https://www.newpractice.net). My work on the series focused on technical production: streaming, sound, camera workflows, and live event setup.

The production system grew out of the StreamingKit developed in InKüLe and was adapted for a more demanding event format with recurring sessions, multi-camera operation, and hybrid distribution.

## Responsibilities

- Multi-platform streaming
- FOH sound and signal flow
- Camera setup and live switching
- Monitor and screen routing
- Wireless microphone management
- Documentation and workflow setup

More than 20 sessions were produced with this setup, including one event at Ars Electronica.

For me, the project was mainly about reliability: building a workflow that artists and organizers could trust while keeping the technical side flexible enough for different event formats.
