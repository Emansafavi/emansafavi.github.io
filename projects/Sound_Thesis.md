# Sound/Thesis

Sound/Thesis was a modular sound synthesis workshop taught with InKüLe at New Practice Studio, TU Berlin, on May 26, 2025.

The session introduced modular synthesis through VCV Rack and was designed for participants without a technical background. The focus was practical: patching, listening, and building simple systems step by step.

## Workshop structure

- Introduction to modular sound synthesis
- Basic modules: oscillators, VCAs, filters, envelopes, LFOs, sequencers
- Effects, MIDI, quantizers, and routing
- Hands-on patching session
- Group presentation and discussion

## Format

- Hybrid event
- In-person workshop with livestream
- Full recording available through the official InKüLe documentation
